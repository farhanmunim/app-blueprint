Apr 14, 2026
Inspector panel and command palette now available

The workspace now includes an Inspector right-panel for editing record properties, managing tags, and reviewing activity without leaving the canvas. Open it from any row, or toggle it with the slim tab on the edge.

We've also rolled out a command palette — hit ⌘K anywhere in the app to jump between workspaces, items, and settings. Learn more about the new shortcuts.

Mar 28, 2026
Permissions, webhook retries, and timezone fixes

A small but important patch release. Admin users can once again write to billing endpoints after we resolved a regression in our permission check. Webhook delivery now honours exponential backoff after rate-limit responses, and date pickers correctly respect the workspace timezone instead of UTC.

AK Aisha K. MR Mo R.
Mar 11, 2026
Console panel, status badges, and a new mobile shell

We've shipped a console panel at the bottom of the workspace for live logs, output, and problems — collapse it with the chevron to reclaim vertical space. Tables now use colour-coded status badges for Active, Pending, and Error states.

On mobile, the shell has been rewritten as an off-canvas drawer with a five-tab bottom navigation, so every tool in the app is one tap away.
